# Welcome

**REALITY (REnew All Living Things In Yeshu) Ministry** aims to prepare the next generation for the era of Artificial Intelligence (AI) through the lens of Christian faith.
It is foreseeable that AI will revolutionize many aspects of our society, including education systems, and significantly impact the formation of personal values.

We anticipate that without proper preparation, our next generation will be swayed by AI systems that increasingly diverge from God's perspective.
Therefore, we call upon the Christian community to unite in developing AI technology that reflects truths from the Bible and the love of Jesus Christ.

By creating trustworthy AI resources, we seek to nurture our children's faith in the one true God, providing them with reliable tools to grow spiritually in this era.
We are committed to bridging the gap between technological advancement and Christian principles, fostering a future where AI serves as an unwavering means to strengthen, not challenge, our relationship with God.

# Our Missions

1. **AI-Enhanced Spiritual Tools** - Develop and disseminate AI-powered applications to enrich Christian life and practice (e.g., interactive Bible study aids, personalized devotional assistants, AI-supported prayer companions, faith community engagement platforms).
2. **Christian Resource Database** - Curate and digitize a comprehensive collection of Christian materials for AI training and reference (e.g., devotionals, theological texts, sermon archives, ministry curricula, Christian literature).
3. **Faith-Aligned Evaluation Framework** - Establish and maintain a faithful evaluation system to ensure AI technologies align with Christian values (e.g., scriptural consistency checks, theological accuracy assessments, ethical decision-making models).
4. **Christian-Centric AI Innovation** - Pioneer foundational AI technologies dedicated for Christian applications, building upon our curated resources (#2) and evaluation framework (#3).
5. **Future Faith-Tech Leaders** - Educate, mentor, and inspire the next generation of Christian technologists, equipping them to lead in the development, application, and evaluation of AI in Chiristian faith.
